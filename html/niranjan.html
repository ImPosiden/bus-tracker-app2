Track Your College Bus in Real Time

Never miss your ride again. Our system lets you view the live location of your college buses, check available seats, and receive notifications when your bus is near your stop.

What is this Website About?

This platform provides real-time GPS tracking of college buses, so students and faculty can plan their journeys with accuracy and convenience. You can check exactly where your bus is and how many seats are left before you step out.

How to Use It?

Step 1: On your laptop, click Track Your Bus in the navigation bar.

Step 2: Select your bus route and watch the live map update in real time.

How It Works?

Our system uses GPS devices installed in college buses to send location updates to our servers. These updates are displayed in real time on our website.

Seat availability is updated by the conductor or automated sensors in the bus, ensuring that the data you see is always accurate and up-to-date.

Why Choose Us?

Unlike generic tracking apps, our platform is tailored specifically for your college routes. You get instant, relevant, and accurate updates without the clutter of irrelevant routes.

We prioritize speed, accuracy, and ease of use so you can focus on your day, not your commute.
